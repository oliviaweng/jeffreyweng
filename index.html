Hello! I am a Ph.D. candidate in sociology at the University of California, Berkeley. My research centers on state formation and nation-building in early 20th-century China. Specifically, my dissertation examines the Chinese state's efforts to standardize Mandarin Chinese in the 1910s, 20s, and 30s.
